# 🚀 PeoplePulse AI

### Enterprise Workforce Intelligence & Predictive Talent Analytics Platform

Transforming HR data into strategic business decisions through Machine Learning, Workforce Analytics, and Executive Intelligence Dashboards.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://enterprise-workforce-intelligence-platform-kqwb6xrttdx7ju47ztk.streamlit.app/

## 🎯 Overview

PeoplePulse AI is an enterprise-grade Workforce Intelligence Platform designed to help organizations predict employee attrition, identify retention risks, monitor workforce health, and drive data-driven HR decisions.

Built as an end-to-end Data Science and Analytics product, the platform combines Machine Learning, Business Intelligence, Data Engineering, and Executive Dashboards into a single solution used to answer one critical question:

> **"Which employees are at risk of leaving, why are they at risk, and what actions should the organization take?"**

## 🧠 Machine Learning Capabilities

The platform evaluates multiple ML models to identify employees at risk of attrition:

* Logistic Regression
* Random Forest
* Gradient Boosting
* XGBoost

Model outputs include:

✅ Attrition Probability

✅ Risk Score (0–100)

✅ Risk Tier Classification

✅ Top Risk Drivers

✅ Explainable Feature Importance
